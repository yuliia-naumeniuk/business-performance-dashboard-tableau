# Business Performance Overview Dashboard
Tableau dashboard analyzing business performance, sales, traffic, and conversion metrics.

## Dashboard Preview
![Dashboard Preview](dashboard.png)

## Business Task
Analyze company performance using sales and website traffic data to identify trends across product categories, countries, devices, and marketing channels.

## Tools
Tableau, Data Visualization, Dashboard Design

## Key Insights
- The Americas generate the highest sales among all regions.
- Sofas & armchairs and chairs are the top-selling product categories.
- Desktop devices generate the majority of sales, while mobile contributes a significant share.
- Organic Search and Direct channels drive most of the traffic and sales.
- Sales show clear fluctuations over time with several noticeable peaks.
